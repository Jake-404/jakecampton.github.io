01 — About
The person behind the platform

Modern communications is much more than messaging — it touches product, partnerships, community, and the C-Suite. Something I know well from my current role, where I head communications and social media at VeChain, a global blockchain foundation with 2M+ followers across multiple channels.

The day-to-day spans copywriting, narrative strategy, partner and product marketing, KOL management, community engagement, executive comms, and agency coordination. I've supported go-to-market for major product launches, with two achieving over 5 million users each — VeWorld wallet (5.5M+ downloads) and VeBetter (5M+ users).

I've represented VeChain as brand spokesperson with 30+ appearances across conferences, podcasts, events, and livestreams — from Innovation Stage panels at the London Blockchain Conference alongside BCG, to solo episodes on Cointelegraph and BeInCrypto. When the CEO isn't available, I step in.

I hold a Master's in Science Communication — a discipline built on clarity, precision, and empathy for the audience. That foundation shows up in everything: from long-form content strategy to a 30-second elevator pitch.

9+ years across blockchain, healthcare, and agency environments. Still energised by the work.
