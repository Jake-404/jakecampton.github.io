01 — About
The person behind the platform

Communications is about making complex things clear and giving people a reason to care. Over nine years — across blockchain, healthcare, and agency environments — that's been the thread running through everything. From early community management to leading comms for a global Layer-1 ecosystem, the work has always centred on the same thing: finding the story inside the product and making it land.

At VeChain, that meant shaping how every major launch was positioned and told — from VeBetter (5M+ users) and VeWorld wallet (5.5M+ downloads) to protocol upgrades and enterprise partnerships with UFC, Nitto ATP Finals, and Boston Consulting Group among many others. It also meant stepping up as brand spokesperson, deputising for the CEO across podcasts, conferences, and livestreams, and managing agencies, KOL programmes, and partner campaigns.

A Master's in Science Communication provides the foundation: clarity, precision, and genuine empathy for the audience. Everything else builds on that.
