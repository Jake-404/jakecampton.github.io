01 — About

The person
behind the platform

I build communications functions that operate at the intersection of technology, culture and human behaviour. Over eight years in blockchain — first as an early believer, then as a builder — I've developed a philosophy centred on creating what I call "woah moments": the instants when complex ideas click, audiences lean in, and brands become memorable.

At VeChain, I transformed a reactive comms function into a proactive, multi-channel operation. I built the global ambassador programme from scratch, grew combined social reach to over 2 million followers, and hosted flagship livestreams alongside the CEO that drew hundreds of thousands of views. I pioneered AI-augmented workflows before they were standard practice, while maintaining the editorial quality and brand voice expected of a global enterprise.

I hold an MSc in Science Communication — a discipline that demands clarity, precision, and empathy for your audience. It's the foundation everything else sits on.
